About Us

TopLink Comprehensive College located at Aladura area,ohunrun olupona was founded in september,2015 by Eng'r Ogunrinu Ismaila Akanbi.Most of the pioneer students and other Alumni are in a various higher institutions studying the course of their choice. Currently the school has over 150 students being nurtured by certified and qualified teachers.TCC is a register and accredited school,a center for External exams such as Basic Education certificate Examination(BECE)
